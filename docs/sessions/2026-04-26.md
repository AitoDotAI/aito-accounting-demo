## Stuck / blocked

- **Full precompute is slow under workers=4**: per-customer time
  spikes from 86 s (sequential) to ~400 s when 4 workers contend on
  the same Aito instance. Net throughput improvement is small
  (~2x). Probably worth profiling whether `_evaluate` and
  `mine_rules_for_customer` serialize on the Aito side. For the
  hosted demo we'd run precompute once during build, so the wall
  time matters but isn't a blocker.

## Aito quirks discovered

- `_evaluate` with `select: [..., "cases"]` returns `case.testCase`
  (full row), `case.accurate` (bool), `case.top.{feature,$p}`,
  `case.correct.{feature,$p}` â€” not `$value` as in `_predict`.
